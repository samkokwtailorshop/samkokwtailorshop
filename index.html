Our Services

At SamKo KW Professional Tailor Shop, we offer a wide range of services to ensure your clothes fit perfectly.

  • Bridesmaid Dress Alterations
    Expert alterations to ensure the perfect fit for bridesmaid dresses, making sure each dress complements the wedding theme and fits beautifully.
  • Drapery & Curtains Alterations
    Tailor your drapes and curtains for the perfect fit and look in your home, with precision hemming and resizing.
  • Kids' Clothing Alterations
    Specialized alterations for children's clothing, ensuring comfort and perfect fit as they grow.
  • Men's Clothing Alterations
    Professional tailoring for men's clothing, including suits, trousers, and shirts, ensuring a sharp, tailored look.
  • Pants Hemming
    Plain hem (invisible hem), polo hem, yoga hem, Euro hem, cuffs.
  • Suit Resizing
    Resize your suits for a perfect fit, whether it's adjusting the jacket, trousers, or overall silhouette.
  • Wedding Gown Alterations
    Shortening, taking in sides, adding sequence, adding pads, taking in back, adding zipper, shortening straps, reshaping armholes, reducing shoulder.
  • Zipper Replacement
    Pants zippers, jackets zippers, dress zippers, skirts zippers, leather zippers, replacing sliders or pulls.
  • Add Buttons, Fix Buttons
    We add and fix buttons on all types of clothing, ensuring durability and a neat appearance.
  • Blouse Alterations
    Shorten sleeves, take in sides, taper arms. Take in back seam. Shorten blouse.
  • Coat & Jacket Alterations
    Shorten sleeves, take in sides, taper arms. Take in back seam. Shorten jacket, reduce shoulder.
  • Dress Alterations
    Shorten length, take in the sides, take in back through zipper, shorten straps.
  • Leather Repair
    Shortening pants, taper the legs, shorten sleeves on a jacket, take in sides.
  • Pants Alterations
    Take in waist, take in sides, taper the legs, take in crotch, add gusset, plain hem, polo hem, Euro hem.
  • Shirt Tailoring
    Take in sides, darts, taper arms, shorten sleeves, shorten the length.
  • Uniform Tailoring
    Alteration on all kinds of uniforms.
  • Women's Clothing Alterations
    Comprehensive alterations for women's clothing, from casual wear to formal attire, ensuring a perfect fit.
  • Prom Dress Alterations
    Shorten dress, take in sides, shorten straps, take in back seam, take in through zipper, shorten lining, reduce shoulder, taper arms, shorten sleeves, take in waist.
  • Body Measurement Services
    Get precise body measurements to ensure a perfect fit for all your tailored clothing.
